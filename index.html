About Me

Teaching Philosophy


My teaching philosophy is rooted in the principles of educational psychology and learner-centered pedagogy. I believe that a well-managed, inclusive classroom fosters both academic excellence and personal growth. As an English teacher, I focus on developing students’ literacy, communication, and critical thinking skills through engaging literature and purposeful language instruction.


By integrating effective teaching methodologies with guidance and counseling principles, I strive to support each learner’s intellectual and emotional development, preparing them to become confident communicators and lifelong learners.
